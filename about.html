About The Colloquium

Shaping Nigeria's future, together.

The Seyi Tinubu Colloquium is an annual platform dedicated to advancing conversations around leadership, innovation, enterprise, youth engagement and national development.

Collaboration across sectors

Established to foster collaboration across sectors and generations, the platform brings together diverse voices committed to shaping Nigeria's future.

A forum for engagement

The Colloquium serves as a forum for engagement, learning and partnership across government, business and civil society.

Practical ideas for growth

It encourages practical ideas and constructive dialogue around the issues affecting the country's growth and development.

Vision

To build a leading platform for dialogue, collaboration and thought leadership that contributes to shaping the future of Nigeria.

Mission

To inspire conversations, partnerships and actions that promote innovation, enterprise, leadership and sustainable national development.
