Welcome

A platform for ideas, engagement and impact.

The Seyi Tinubu Colloquium is an annual platform established to inspire meaningful conversations on leadership, innovation, enterprise, youth participation and national development.

It convenes policymakers, business leaders, innovators, creatives, development partners and emerging leaders to explore ideas and opportunities capable of shaping a stronger and more prosperous Nigeria.
